Chapter · I

The Story

01

A framed gamble

After a runaway gamble, Iris is recorded as the sole person responsible.

However, she was just an agent gambler who bet on behalf of others, but overnight she was burdened with huge debts, and was identified by the system as the main culprit.

In order to prove her innocence, she had to return to the closed and luxurious private casino.

A place that devours secrets and lies.

02

Find the truth in traps

Neon corridors, closed rooms, people talking in low voices.

From the lobby to the internal facility in the casino, there may be clues hidden everywhere.

To ask friend, J, or other employees to get more help. Observe the environment, solve puzzles,

get closer to the truth step by step, or fall deeper into a scam.

03

Trust is the most dangerous bargaining chip

Here, friendship just be another form of transaction.

You will face betrayal, falling into self-doubt.

Then, gradually see the temptation behind gambling. When trust is in crisis,

how will you choose?

The Lost Gambler

After losing her job, her life began to crumble, leading her to cut ties with her past social circles and lose her sense of direction. She was once relatively lucky, enjoying buying scratch cards after work. Now, she lives cautiously on her dwindling savings, yet an impulsive desire for a fresh start still lingers in her heart. Her life changes again when she receives a message from an acquaintance, J, who invites her to work as an enforcer in a private casino. She gambles on behalf of clients but is framed after a failed game, burdened with an unbearable debt. The player's goal is to play as her, explore the truth of the casino, and find a way out of this desperate situation.

He has a fit appearance and a flamboyant, attention-grabbing dress sense. His speech and demeanor carry a familiar, affable quality; he is adept at dealing with all kinds of people, familiar with numerous floor managers and dealers, and has extensive interpersonal connections. J's main source of income is introducing gamblers as a proxy, taking commissions to make a living. He appears warm on the surface but is actually cold and utilitarian at heart, willing to sacrifice others without hesitation once his core interests are involved. It is he who draws Iris into the scheme and later conceals part of the truth.
